# 🔴🔵 FCB Guwahati News Bot

Welcome to the home of the official news bot for the **FCB Guwahati** Discord server! 

This bot acts as our dedicated, 24/7 automated reporter. Its only job is to scour the web for the latest FC Barcelona news and deliver it straight to our community channel so we never miss an update about our beloved club.

## ✨ What Does It Do?

* **Instant Updates:** Every 15 minutes, the bot checks trusted Barcelona news sources for new articles, match updates, and transfer rumors.
* **Beautiful Blaugrana Cards:** Instead of dropping messy links, the bot formats every piece of news into a clean, easy-to-read card featuring the article's title, a short preview, a picture, and the official FCB Carmine Red color.
* **No Spam:** The bot has a built-in memory. It tracks exactly what it has already shared to guarantee that we never get annoyed by duplicate posts.
* **Always Awake:** Hosted seamlessly in the cloud, it works flawlessly day and night so the news is always waiting for you.

## 📰 Where Does The News Come From?

Currently, the bot brings us fresh, reliable content directly from:
* **Everything Barca**
* **Barca Blaugranes**
